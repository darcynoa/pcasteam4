Pedestrian Collision
Avoidance System

The world of technology is in an era that is researching and testing more and more autonomous devices that make day to day tasks more convenient. Devices like our Pedestrian Collision Avoidance System on a vehicle will take us to further depths of autonomous driving.
The Pedestrian Collision Avoidance System (PCAS) is an embedded system that scans its environemnt through different sensors and radars that help with sensing obstacles through heavy weather and dark nights. The PCAS should be able to detect an imminent collision, slow down gradually, or use other means to avoid the collision, and start to speed up once the obstacle has moved out of range.
